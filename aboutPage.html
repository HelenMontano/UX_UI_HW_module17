I'm captivated by design's ability to evoke emotions, and this passion has led me to embark on a transformative journey to become a designer. The experience has been a continuous, overwhelming, and exhilarating learning process. While I acknowledge that I am still on the learning curve, it's precisely this dynamic nature of the field that draws me in - design is a constant evolution, demanding continuous research and learning.

I have a Bachelor's degree in Media & Cultural Stuides and Sociology from the University of California, Riverside. I come from a background in education as a substitute teacher and healthcare as a caregiver, I've found fulfillment in these roles. However, I am now poised for a new challenge, and design is the exciting and vibrant path I am eager to traverse and grow within.

When I'm not crafting user experiences, you'll find me lost in the pages of a captivating book, freezing time through the lens of one of my cameras, savoring precious moments with my loved ones, or curating a playlist for my radio show. Designing is my professional love, but these personal passions are the vibrant colors that paint the canvas of my life.
